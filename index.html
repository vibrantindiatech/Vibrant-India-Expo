Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the exhibition

Who should exhibit at Vibrant India 2026?

Vibrant India 2026 is ideal for manufacturers, suppliers, and distributors of ice cream, chocolate, cold drinks, processing equipment, packaging solutions, ingredients, and related products and services. Both domestic and international companies are welcome.

What are the exhibition timings?

The exhibition will be open from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM from July 17-20, 2026. The venue is Gujarat University Convention and Exhibition Centre in Ahmedabad.

How can I register as a visitor?

Visitor registration will open in early 2026. You can pre-register on our website to receive updates and early access to registration. Entry is free for pre-registered trade visitors.

What safety measures are in place?

We prioritize the safety of all participants. The venue will have comprehensive safety protocols including emergency medical services, security personnel, and clear evacuation routes. All exhibitors must comply with safety regulations.

Location & Directions

The Vibrant India 2026 exhibition will be held at the Gujarat University Convention and Exhibition Centre in Ahmedabad, one of India's most modern and well-equipped exhibition venues.

By Air

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport (AMD) is approximately 15 km from the venue. Taxis and app-based cabs are readily available.

By Train

Ahmedabad Railway Station is about 8 km from the venue. Regular local transport connects the station to the exhibition centre.

By Road

Ahmedabad is well-connected by national highways. The venue has ample parking space for visitors arriving by car.
